# Release status

The former August 2025 “final report” was retired. Its performance and accuracy
claims had no reproducible evidence and the old CLI contained release-blocking bugs.

The current v2.1 release criteria are objective: strict configuration, read-only
operation, eight deterministic daily recommendations, optional non-authoritative
model review, green CI/security gates, daily scheduling, structured evidence-bearing
alerts, deduplication, and documented limitations.

Independent pre-release review additionally verified effective risk-cap propagation,
non-zero monotonic realized volatility, evidence-domain quality reconciliation,
resolved-incident polarity, full model audit metadata, serialized delivery, and
preservation of the first daily digest during same-day suppression.
